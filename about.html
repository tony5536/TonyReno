About

I'm a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. With expertise spanning fraud detection, healthcare AI, computer vision, and predictive analytics, I transform complex data into actionable insights.

My journey began with a fascination for patterns—how seemingly random data could reveal profound truths. This curiosity led me to specialize in machine learning, where I've developed systems that detect financial fraud with 99.2% accuracy, predict healthcare outcomes with 94% precision, and enable gesture-based robot control with 43% efficiency gains.

What drives me is the intersection of technical excellence and human impact. Every model I build, every algorithm I optimize, serves a purpose beyond metrics—it protects financial systems, enhances campus security, improves patient outcomes, and makes technology more accessible through innovations like sign language translation.

I believe in open-source collaboration, having contributed to projects with over 2.3K GitHub stars, and in continuous learning, constantly exploring the latest advances in deep learning, computer vision, and AI ethics. My work spans multiple domains, from fintech to healthcare to robotics, always with a focus on creating solutions that are both powerful and responsible.
